### Understand Tax Brackets
Progressive tax = Different rates on different portions of income

Example (USA):
```
$0-$11,000: 10% = $1,100
$11,000-$44,725: 12% = $4,047
$44,725-$50,000: 22% = $1,160.50

Total on $50K = $6,307.50 (12.6% effective)
```
